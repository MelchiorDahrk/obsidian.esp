Admirers of the unarmed, [[unarmored]] [[martial arts]] traditions of the Marshmerrow, Salt Rice, and Golden Reed societies have adapted them for use in the [[Mages Guild]], in the criminal subcultures, and among opponents of [[Imperial]] occupation of [[Morrowind]].
