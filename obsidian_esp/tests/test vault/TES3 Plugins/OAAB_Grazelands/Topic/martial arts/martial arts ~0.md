The unarmed, [[unarmored]] [[martial arts]] traditions of the Marshmerrow, Salt Rice, and Golden Reed societies of the [[Dissident Priests]], patterned on the 'Rain-of-Sand' fighting styles of [[Elsweyr]], are slow to be adopted in [[the Empire]], associated as they are with the ascetic renunciation of worldly wealth and material goods, and the rigid disciplines and mystical philosophies so alien to the [[Imperial]] West.
