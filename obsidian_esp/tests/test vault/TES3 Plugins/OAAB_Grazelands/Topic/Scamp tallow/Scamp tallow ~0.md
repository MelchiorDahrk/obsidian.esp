The candles surrounding an enchanter's table aren't just for show. Skilled [[enchanters]] can use the aura cast by particular candles to create better [[enchantments]]. In the West -- especially in [[High Rock]] -- magetallow is frequently used. And some Telvanni use it as well. But scamp tallow candles are almost as powerful and much more... tastefully acquired. [[Scamp tallow]] is rendered from the fat left clumped under scamp skin. Magetallow, well, I'll let you think about how it is made. ^obsidian-esp-canvas-40207123e1
