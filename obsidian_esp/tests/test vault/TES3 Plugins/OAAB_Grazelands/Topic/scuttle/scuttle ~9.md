[[Dunmer]] food is unusual and frequently off-putting to outlanders like us. Seems that they've found a way to turn almost anything that moves into food. But some of the strangest dishes can actually be quite delectable if you're adventurous enough to try it. Scuttle is one of those things. It is [[Vvardenfell]]'s favorite local food and resembles cheese in texture. The taste is something unique that you just have to try for yourself. As I mentioned, I can offer you a lifetime supply of [[scuttle]].
