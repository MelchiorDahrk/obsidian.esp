In ancient times, [[Molaguradan]] was a burial place for several tribes which lived in the area, including the [[Salkurnudai]]. In modern times, we entomb our dead by other means, but the fiery caverns of [[Molaguradan]] are still a [[sacred]] place. Unfortunately, we do not have access to the caverns now that the Telvanni lord built his tower atop it. ^obsidian-esp-canvas-ddafb8d950
